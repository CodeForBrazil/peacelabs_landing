Creating projects all together

PeaceLabs is a lab where people come together to co create social projects. Here, creators, volunteers and social investors meet to bennefit from the power of crowdsourcing and technology to improve the engagement and return on investments of their initiatives.

Ideas

  • What is peace for you?
  • Which social, environmental or economical solution you want to develop?
  • What problem do you think that humanity must solve today?

Skills

  • You are a designer, programmer or project manager? Or maybe a writer, marketer and networker?
  • Do you know how to do something better than others?
  • What do you love to do and believe it can be of value to others?

Money

  • You are a social project enthusiast? Enjoy crowdfunding projects online?
  • Do you you rather invest your money in solutions that improve our world?
  • Do you know any company that wants to optimize their social investments?
